Smart Fire Alert

The Arduino-based Fire Alert System is designed to detect fire or smoke in your home and immediately notify the house owner via SMS. Using a flame or smoke sensor, the system continuously monitors for any signs of a fire. Once detected, the system triggers an alarm by sounding a buzzer and flashing an LED for immediate attention. At the same time, it sends a real-time SMS message to the homeowner, providing crucial information about the emergency situation.
